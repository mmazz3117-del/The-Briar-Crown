The Briar Crown v1.7.2.2 — Clear Scene & Map Rebuild

This build integrates the bright, clear scene direction approved in July 2026.

Highlights
- New bright 4:3 scene art for the village, shops, forge, chapel route, forest chain, cottage, Moonfen, Moonwell, passages, alcove, and crypt.
- New high-resolution world map with revised discovered-node coordinates.
- Directional scenes now use 4:3 framing instead of the previous wide crop that caused close-ups.
- The extra dark scene filter is disabled for the rebuilt artwork.
- Visible NPC/item markers keep Merrin, Elowen, the innkeeper, animals, coins, keys, charms, and quest items easy to find and tap.
- All active scene paths use v1722 filenames to prevent stale PWA caching.

Note
The tavern directional set, chapel interior, Ironthorn Gate, Moonfen hut exterior, and tavern cellar were carefully upscaled and enhanced from the strongest existing production scenes because a matching final fresh render was not available in the approved art batch. All other active backgrounds use the newly approved scene direction.
