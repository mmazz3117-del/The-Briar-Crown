A Proclamation from Willowmere

The Crown Is Missing

For generations, the Briar Crown rested within Ironthorn Castle, binding the oldest and most dangerous magic beneath the land.

Seven days ago, the Crown vanished. Since then, forgotten roads have shifted, wild creatures have grown restless, and sealed tombs have begun to open.

No messenger sent to Ironthorn has returned. Strange relics are surfacing near Willowmere, and something ancient is stirring beneath Saint Orla’s chapel.

Your journey begins in Willowmere Square. Follow the clues, recover what was scattered, and discover who took the Crown before the awakened magic consumes the realm.
